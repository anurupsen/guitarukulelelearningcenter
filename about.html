Unveiling Our Musical Journey and Passion

From rock music of the West to the folk from the heart of Bangladesh, here you can master anything you want! Having years of experience playing as a professional guitarist, Rahamat Ullah founded "Guitar & Ukulele Learning Center" on 10 September, 2022. Jannatul Ferdous , our female instructor and co-founder, has been playing a vital role in making guitar and ukulele learning more accessible to the female learners in this Bangladeshi socio-cultural context.

After starting from Dhaka University campus, soon we had to add two more of our branches in Jagannath University campus and Matuail, Jatrabari. We offer both offline and online classes. Besides if you prefer learning guitar staying in the comfort of your home( Inside Dhaka city), we can provide you with male or female home tutor according to your preference. So, if you are looking for any guidance to learn guitar & ukulele from the very beginning or as an intermediate learner, just get in touch with us and together we'll explore the endless realms of musicalÂ brilliance!
