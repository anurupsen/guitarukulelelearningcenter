frequently asked questions

How many classes do you offer/month?

our institution offer 4 classes per month in online private classes, offline private & batch classes.

How much fees do you take for all classes/month?

For our offline Batches classes we take 1000/- per month
For our offline private classes we take 2000/- per month
For our online private classes we take 1000/- per month
For Home Tuition Monthly Fee Depends On Your Location. Please Contact For Details.

** The first class of every month is to be paid with the monthly fee.

How I will get admitted to your courses?

You may visit our Website, facebook page & Instagram page and from there you can fill our form and also contact us.
** Admission should be confirmed by paying the admission fee of 500 Taka.

Where you conduct your offline batch classes?

we have 3 branches in dhaka City
1. Dhaka University Campus
2. Jagannath University Campus
3. Matuail, Jatrabari.

How I will pay you?

Students Can pay us through Bkash
you will get our Bkash number when you will contact us.
