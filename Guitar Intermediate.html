Intermediate Guitar Course



Course Duration: 7 month
Course Plan:
1. Advance finger exercises
2. Advance plucking style with finger
3. Different Scales (Major, Minor, Pentatonic, Blues)
4. Different modes (Ionian, Dorian, Phrygian, lydian etc..)
5. Different types of chords [sus 2 , sus 4, Added chords, 7th chords (major, minor, dominat)]
6. Riff
7. How to use chord families
8. Advance Strumming Patterns & Rhythms
9. How to play solo on guitar
